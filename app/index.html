culture
Our construction process ensures superior quality and enduring value. Our construction team delivers professionalism, attention to detail, and service in the field. We are always looking several steps ahead to anticipate issues and keep abreast of anything that might influence the cost, schedule, or quality of our work. Our team is adept at carefully coordinating construction activities to minimize disturbances to adjacent tenants or building systems. We use our TI expertise to facilitate the continuous flow of work, and our onsite superintendents provide clients and the project team with immediate access to information and status. Our field teams have some of the most TI and construction experience in the industry, which enables them to anticipate project needs and seek proactive solutions. Axmis Construction field professionals value craftsmanship and delivering beautifully finished spaces to our clients.

Quality Commitment
AXMIS equips every individual performing work in the field with the information and tools necessary to execute properly the first time. Prior to construction, our superintendent creates a project-specific quality plan that details critical design elements, quality details and unique challenges that will need to be addressed in the work, and then holds each individual accountable for achieving the established quality standards, from early verification through final inspection. Our disciplined process prevents quality issues before they arise.
